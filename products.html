Our Products

Ultraviolet specializes in cutting-edge products that leverage Confidential Computing technology to address the evolving needs of organizations. Our suite of products empowers businesses with secure, collaborative, and privacy-preserving solutions, enabling them to harness the full potential of their data while safeguarding confidentiality and integrity.

Cocos AI

Cocos AI empowers secure collaboration on AI projects and confidential data sharing through robust Secure Multi-party Computation (SMPC) capabilities within Trusted Execution Environments (TEEs). It provides organizations and developers with a versatile platform for executing confidential workloads, ensuring the privacy and security of sensitive data during collaborative AI endeavors.

Learn More

EOS

EOS (Enclave Operating System) represents an upcoming product in active development as part of the CONFIDENTIAL6G project. In collaboration with Ultraviolet, a key partner, this initiative focuses on advancing the field of Confidential Computing technology, with a strong emphasis on elevating data security and privacy standards.

Learn More

TEE-V

TEE-V is an open-source, RISC-V-based custom Trusted Execution Environment (TEE) meticulously designed by Ultraviolet's expert team. With a strong focus on applications such as collaborative AI, Secure Multi-party Computation (SMPC), and High-Performance Computing (HPC), TEE-V offers a versatile and secure solution for advanced computing needs.
